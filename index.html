What is OTU?

OTU stands for Operational Taxonomic Unit.

OTU is an operational definition used to classify groups of closely related individuals (the individuals in question are the different bacteria cultures we are charting).

The gauge corresponds to how frequently the Test Subjects ID is washing their belly button per week.

The bubble chart above is a different way to view our bar graph. Each bubble on the chart describes a particular OTU and the size of each bubble corresponds to how much of that bacteria is present in the belly button of the Test Subject's ID.
